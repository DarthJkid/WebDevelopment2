About Us

Who We Are

The National Centre for Computing Education (NCCE) is funded by the Department for Education and supporting partners and marks a significant investment in improving the provision of computing education in England.

These Hub supports schools and colleges in Peterborough to provide a high-quality computing education to all young people. We work with you to identify your computing needs and provide a targeted programme of support to help embed this key subject in your school.

We provide teachers with a comprehensive range of evidence-informed, high-quality support, including free resources and training courses. Funding is available, including subsidies, to support supply cover for continuing professional development - to help them become inspirational computing teachers.
