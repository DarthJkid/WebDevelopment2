Skilled Instructors

Our instructors are highly trained professionals with extensive experience in their respective fields. They are dedicated to providing the best education and support to help you achieve your learning goals.

Online Classes

Our online classes offer flexibility and convenience, allowing you to learn at your own pace from the comfort of your home. With a wide range of courses available, you can enhance your skills and knowledge in various subjects.

On-Site Facilities

Our on-site facilities provide a conducive learning environment with state-of-the-art equipment. We offer face-to-face courses to ensure personalized instruction and hands-on experience to enhance teaching skills.

Certification

Our certification programs are designed to validate your skills and knowledge in various subjects. Upon successful completion of our courses, you will receive a certificate that is recognized and valued by employers worldwide.

About Us

Who We Are

The National Centre for Computing Education (NCCE) is funded by the Department for Education and supporting partners and marks a significant investment in improving the provision of computing education in England.

Opened in 2025, This Hub supports schools and colleges in Peterborough to provide a high-quality computing education to all young people. We will work with you to identify your computing needs and provide a targeted programme of support to help embed this key subject in your school.

We provide teachers with a comprehensive range of evidence-informed, high- quality support, including free resources and training courses. Funding is available, including subsidies, to support supply cover for continuing professional development - to help them become inspirational computing teachers
